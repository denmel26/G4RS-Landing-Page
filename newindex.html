About Project G4RS

  • About the Project

    Project G4RS is an innovative initiative spearheaded by the University of the Cordilleras in collaboration with the Baguio City Police Office (BCPO) and the Local Government Units (LGUs) of Baguio City. Funded by the Department of Science and Technology – Philippine Council for Industry, Energy and Emerging Technology Research and Development (DOST-PCIEERD), this project uses cutting-edge Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and data analytics to mitigate road accidents in Baguio City.

    The primary focus of Project G4RS is to analyze traffic accident data, identify accident-prone areas, and provide a decision-support platform for informed infrastructure planning and policymaking.

  • Key Objectives

    Accident Detection & Prevention: Using GIS and data analytics to identify high-risk areas for traffic accidents.

    Data-Driven Decision Making: Applying time-series and spatial analysis to assist in policy formation and city infrastructure planning.

    Road Safety Enhancement: Aimed at reducing traffic-related accidents to improve public safety and promote sustainable urban mobility.

  • Our Approach

    We combine historical accident data with real-time monitoring to identify patterns and predict potential accident hotspots. Our multi-faceted approach includes engineering improvements, public awareness campaigns, and policy recommendations.

    Through collaboration with local authorities and community stakeholders, we implement targeted interventions that have demonstrated measurable results in reducing accidents and saving lives.

Road Safety Initiatives

Hotspot Identification

Using advanced GIS technology to pinpoint high-risk areas and prioritize interventions where they're needed most.

Data Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of accident patterns, contributing factors, and temporal trends to inform safety measures.

Public Awareness

Educational campaigns and community engagement programs to promote road safety awareness among all users.

Policy Recommendations

Evidence-based suggestions for policy improvements and infrastructure changes to enhance road safety.

Impact Monitoring

Continuous evaluation of implemented measures to assess effectiveness and make data-driven adjustments.

Stakeholder Collaboration

Working closely with government agencies, community leaders, and residents to implement comprehensive solutions.
