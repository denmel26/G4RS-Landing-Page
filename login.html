PROJECT G4RS

"Safer Roads Today, Stronger Communities Tomorrow."

Project G4RS strives to create safer road networks that promote a healthier and more economically robust Baguio City. Through this initiative, we aim to reduce vehicular accidents and foster a culture of road safety across all road users.
  • Comprehensive accident data analysis
  • Real-time traffic monitoring
  • Interactive maps and visualizations
  • Detailed reporting system
  • 2016-2025 historical data
  • Location-based insights
